SongSpy

About

SongSpy uses advanced audio extraction techniques to train and learn patterns specific to different artists. The model can accurately classify (currently 85%) the artist behind a given piece of music using trained Mel Spectrogram segmentations. The model will also compare the song to the Artist's current catalogue to determine if it is artificially generated. Artists trained on the model include 1) Drake 2) Juice WRLD 3) Kanye West 4) Post Malone 5) SZA 6) Taylor Swift 7) Travis Scott 8) Lil Uzi Vert 9) The Weekend.
